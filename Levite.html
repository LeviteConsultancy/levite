About Levite

Advocate Shine Thomas
Speaks

I view the AI revolution as the next seismic shift, comparable in impact to the internet and telecommunication revolution. Recognizing this impending transformation, I made a major strategic move in 2021: I stepped away from my Senior Vice President role at a leading specialty insurance MNC to invest intensely in realigning myself with the coming age embedded in AI.

My two-decade career is built on deep operational familiarity with financial and insurance sectors, starting my career with a successful startup and then working for 12 years in a leading MNC. This enabled me to cover the entire administration spectrum, from product design to large claims (including property, liabilities, and niche financial products). This background, validated by my Fellowship in Insurance (FIII), makes me a seasoned professional in complex dynamic financial situations.

Now, as a consultant and registered advocate, I focus on bridging this industry knowledge with strategic technology for the larger good. I leverage my experience and extensive network to ensure customers are empowered by technology, not victimized by its misapplication. I believe the future lies in combining specialized human insight with AI's power.

We are a new-age global entity built on a lean concept of a purely online presence.
We focus exclusively on the quality of expertise, approaching each assignment as a project and constituting custom teams of world-class talent tailored to identify holistic solutions.
And most importantly with AI at the core of everything we do.

Success Story: Victory Over Delays and Unjust Charges

After a tragic accident, a total loss claim remained stalled for years due to a pending court judgment. Once documents were finally submitted, the insurer employed stalling tactics and attempted to deduct arbitrary "salvage values" while alleging missing parts. Furthermore, the dealership demanded exorbitant parking fees of ₹500 per day for the four-year duration. Through strategic intervention, we compelled the insurer to abandon intimidation tactics, and successfully secured a full settlement. Most importantly, we ensured the dealership completely waived all parking charges, delivering a comprehensive win for the claimant.

Individual Empowerment
Success Story: Championing Justice for the Vulnerable

When an underprivileged worker was mis-sold a high-premium life insurance policy through systemic deception—falsifying his education and doubling his reported income—he faced financial ruin. Acting pro-bono, we intervened to challenge this predatory "void ab initio" contract. By systematically escalating the matter through formal grievances and leadership channels, we forced a full investigation. The result was a complete victory: the provider admitted the lapse, cancelling the policy and issuing a full refund with interest. This success reaffirms that with persistent advocacy, the rights of the common man can prevail against corporate negligence.
